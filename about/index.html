Bio

Born and raised in Beijing, China, I received my B.S. in Theoretical & Applied Mechanics from Shanghai University. I am a The Third Middle School of BNU ‘17 and RDFZ ‘14. During middle school, I cast a short movie called A Special Lunch in 2013 for Operation Empty Plate. I also played for the class team and won the RDFZ 2013 basketball competition championship. I grew up and recieved my primary education in Experimental Primary School of BNU, in which I started learning playing tuba, doing arts and playing magic tricks.

Interested in fluid mechanics, deep learning, soft materials & biomechanics, I conducted research on mechanical properties of enamel microstructure with Dongsheng Zhang & Dwayne D. Arola from 2018 to 2020, during which we observe a specific band structure that may proves to resist crack verified through simulations. Later on, I worked with Guohui Hu on developing deep learning algorithms applied to microfluids, specifically on microbubble flows. My previous undergraduate research projects can be viewed in my research.

During my four years as a undergraduate, I played Denfensive End & Linebacker at Bombers Football Team from 2017 to 2019 (Football Collection). And I’ve trained with Russell Wilson at RW3 Passing Academy in 2018 (Media coverage on AFInt). I have won the 1st and 3rd place at SHU bodybuilding contest in 2018 and 2019. I have experience in financial training at Bank of China Life at Hong Kong on Feburary 2018. I also love painting, arts, and all sorts of things that excite me. In my spare time, I love watching movies and hit the gym, and I also love history, philosophy, and social science.
